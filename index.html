Privacy Policy for Doha Prayer Times

Last Updated: [add date]

Thank you for using Doha Prayer Times. Your privacy is important to us, and we want you to clearly understand what information we collect, how it’s used, and why.

1. Information We Collect

Doha Prayer Times does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app does not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email
  • Your phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Cookies or analytics data

You can use the app completely without sharing any personal details.

2. Third-Party Service (Al-Adhan API)

To show accurate prayer times for Doha, Qatar, our app uses the Al-Adhan API:

https://api.aladhan.com/v1/timingsByCity?city=Doha&country=Qatar&method=4

This API only provides publicly available prayer time data. We do not send any personal information to the API—only the fixed city name “Doha” is used. You can review Al-Adhan’s own policy on their official website.

3. How We Use the Data

The app only displays:

  • Today’s prayer times
  • The next upcoming prayer time

No data is stored, tracked, or shared by us.

4. Children’s Privacy

Doha Prayer Times does not target children specifically, and since we do not collect personal information, the app is safe for all age groups.

5. Data Security

Since we do not store or transmit any user data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed. All network requests are only made to fetch public prayer time data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. Any updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
